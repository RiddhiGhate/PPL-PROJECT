A crime website is a website that deals specifically with crime or careers . Many crime websites are designed to allow employers to post job requirements for a position to be filled and are commonly known as job boards. Other crime sites offer employer reviews, career and job-search advice, and describe different job descriptions or employers . Through a job website a prospective employee can locate and fill out a job application or submit resumes over the Internet for the advertised position. The Online Career Centre was developed as a non-profit organization backed by forty major corporations to allow job hunters to post their resumes and for recruiters to post job openings.
